Walnut blasting explained

What is walnut blasting and why do modern engines need it?

On modern direct-injection petrol engines, fuel is injected straight into the combustion chamber ‚Äì not over the intake valves. That means there‚Äôs nothing to wash away oil vapour and carbon deposits. Over time, thick carbon builds up on the valve stems and ports, choking the engine.

How our walnut blasting process works

We use crushed walnut shell media, fired at controlled pressure, to gently strip carbon from the intake valves and ports. It‚Äôs abrasive enough to remove stubborn deposits, but safe for metal surfaces when used correctly.
