Common signs linked to intake carbon build-up

  • •Rough idle, hesitation or flat spots under load
  • •Poor throttle response or reduced performance
  • •Misfire symptoms or uneven running on direct-injection engines
  • •Loss of smoothness on higher-mileage engines
  • •Visible heavy deposits during intake inspection or borescope checks

Specialist walnut blasting and intake carbon cleaning in Halesowen

FAQ

Questions customers usually ask before booking

This section helps visitors make a sensible decision and gives Google more context about what the page actually covers.

What is walnut blasting?+

Walnut blasting is a method of removing carbon deposits from intake valves and ports using crushed walnut shell media. It is especially relevant on direct-injection engines where fuel does not wash the back of the valves in the way older systems did.

How do I know if my engine may need walnut blasting?+

Typical signs include hesitation, rough idle, flat spots, reduced response, misfire-like symptoms or heavy deposits visible on inspection. Many customers come to us after research or after being advised by another garage.

Do you work on petrol and diesel engines?+

Yes. Most enquiries are for direct-injection petrol engines, but selected diesel intake and EGR-related cases may also be suitable depending on the layout and the nature of the build-up.

What add-ons can be done at the same time?+

Common add-ons include intake manifold cleaning, EGR cleaning, throttle body cleaning, and ultrasonic injector cleaning for suitable petrol injectors. Add-ons are from £99 per item.

Do you offer diagnosis as well?+

Yes, but as a separate service. Fault diagnosis can be booked separately at £109 per hour.

Do I need to book in advance?+

Yes. Visits are strictly by appointment only, so please contact the workshop before travelling.
