Services

What we do

Carbon build-up on intake valves and ports is common on modern engines, especially direct injection. When airflow drops, drivability often follows: hesitation, flat spots, rough idle and loss of response.

Walnut blasting (core service)

We remove the intake manifold, position valves correctly, and clean each port with controlled walnut media while extracting debris. This is mechanical, targeted cleaning of the area that chemical sprays usually fail to reach.

FAQ

Common questions

Clear answers without sales fluff.

What engines do you cover?

3-cylinder up to V10 and Bentley W12 (access-dependent). We confirm feasibility by engine code/layout before booking.

Petrol direct injection and diesel?

Yes. Petrol DI intake valves/ports, plus suitable diesel intake/EGR restriction work where appropriate.

Is walnut blasting safe?

Yes when done correctly: manifold off, valves closed, controlled media pressure and continuous extraction.

How long does it take?

Typical turnaround is next working day, depending on access and seized fixings.

Will it fix my misfires/hesitation?

If intake carbon is the cause, it can help restore airflow and smoothness. We scan first and advise honestly.
